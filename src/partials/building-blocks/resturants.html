The Harness

The Harness is an exquisite addition to the landscape of the riding club in Granada Hills Rawalpindi. Lavishly designed, The Harness is a unique facility tagged as an upscale café, restaurant and bar. With spectacular interiors, smooth wood and stone finishes, and a luxurious environment make it one of the most exclusive places in the project.

The Harness caters especially to the members of the Granada Hills Rawalpindi riding club, along with any walk in customers with its delicious mixture of culinary delights. From savory starters to mouthful main courses, saddle lounge offers the best food choices in town. Uniquely designed furniture enhances the overall ambience and makes the dining experience even more incredible. The Harness is an extremely tastefully designed facilities built with contemporary styling and are the ultimate place for members to socialize, relax and enjoy their meal.
